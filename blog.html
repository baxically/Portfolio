How did your idea of robotics change?

I have always believed that robots were electronics that were programmed to do certain things. I was never really concerned about them becoming smarter than humans. Due to our research today, however, I am a little concerned about it now. Things like self-driving cars and Sophia scare me a bit.

What makes you excited about the future of robotics? Nervous?

I excited for robots that can help people with disabilities! I am, however, worried that they will overrun the world and make the humanrace even more lazy.

You worked on circuits in groups, and will be working in groups for the rest of the week. What worked well? What could you do better next time?

I really liked my group! They were all very cooperative and easy to talk to. I kind of regret that we decided to work around two tables. There was too much space and we couldn't all see the robot.
